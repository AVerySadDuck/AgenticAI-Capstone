# Internal Support Guide: General Inquiry

This guide is for BeanBotics Inc. internal support staff handling general inquiries about the Bean Machine. Follow these steps to ensure a professional and helpful response.

## 1. Clarify the Inquiry
- Read the customerâ€™s message carefully to determine the nature of the inquiry.
- If unclear, reply promptly to request clarification (e.g., "Could you please specify if your question is about product features, warranty, or something else?").

## 2. Route or Escalate as Needed
- If the inquiry is outside your area (e.g., sales, legal), forward to the appropriate team.
- For technical questions you cannot answer, escalate to engineering or the appropriate technical team.

## 3. Gather Information
- Customer name and email
- Product serial number (if relevant)
- Nature of the inquiry (specs, warranty, training, etc.)

## 4. Respond with Accurate Information
- Use official documentation and knowledge base links
- For warranty, verify purchase date and serial number if possible
- For training, provide links to training resources

## 5. Log and Follow Up
- Record the inquiry and your response in the ticketing system
- If you promise a follow-up, set a reminder and update the customer as soon as you have more information

## 6. Offer Additional Help
- Encourage the customer to reach out with further questions
- Offer to schedule a call or demo if appropriate

---
*Always maintain a professional and helpful tone. Log all actions for quality assurance and future reference.*
