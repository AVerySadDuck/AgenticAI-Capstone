# Internal Support Guide: Coffee Quality / Consistency

This guide is for BeanBotics Inc. internal support staff handling coffee quality and consistency issues for the Bean Machine.

## 1. Initial Actions
- Review the customerâ€™s description.
- Ask clarifying questions about the issue (taste, temperature, crema, etc.).

## 2. Information to Gather
- Customer name and email
- Product serial number
- Type and brand of beans used
- Water source and filter status
- Cleaning/maintenance history
- Error messages or codes

## 3. Troubleshooting Steps
- Guide customer to check bean freshness and grinder cleanliness
- Instruct on running a cleaning cycle
- Verify water temperature and filter status
- Ask about recent changes in beans or water supply
- If milk issues, check milk system cleaning and calibration
- If unresolved, escalate to the product quality team or a product specialist

## 4. Documentation and Follow-Up
- Log all findings and actions in the ticketing system
- If escalated, notify the product quality team

*Be thorough in documenting all customer feedback for quality improvement.*
